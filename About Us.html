About UniMatch

Empowering students to make informed decisions about their educational future through comprehensive university matching and guidance.

Our Story

UniMatch was born from a simple observation: choosing the right university shouldn't be overwhelming or confusing. Founded in 2025, we recognized that students in Botswana and across Africa needed a centralized platform to discover, compare, and connect with universities that truly match their aspirations.

What started as a small project to help local students has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of students annually. We've partnered with leading universities across Botswana to provide accurate, up-to-date information about programs, admission requirements, and career outcomes.

Our founders, having experienced the challenges of university selection firsthand, were determined to create a solution that would democratize access to higher education information and empower every student to find their perfect academic home.

🎓

Our Mission

To simplify the university selection process by providing students with comprehensive, accurate, and easily accessible information about higher education opportunities, enabling them to make informed decisions that align with their academic goals and career aspirations.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational guidance platform in Africa, where every student has access to the tools and information needed to pursue higher education and achieve their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.
