General Admission Information

Standard Entry: Minimum 34 points in best six BGCSE subjects (varies by program - some require up to 38 points)
Language Requirement: English Language proficiency with minimum grade C for all programs
Science Prerequisites: Mathematics & Science subjects required for most programs with specific grade requirements
Competitive Programs: Higher points required for BVM (38 points) and Engineering programs (36 points)
Advanced Entry: Diploma holders may apply for advanced entry with credit transfer opportunities
Specialization Focus: All programs emphasize practical application in agriculture, natural resources, and related fields
