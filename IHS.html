About IHS

The Institute of Health Sciences (IHS) is Botswana's premier health education institution, dedicated to training healthcare professionals who will strengthen the country's health system. Established in 1967, IHS operates multiple campuses across Botswana, providing essential education and training for nursing and allied health professionals.

Working in close partnership with the Ministry of Health, IHS programs are designed to meet national healthcare workforce needs while adhering to international standards of health education. The institute's practical approach to learning combines classroom instruction with extensive clinical experience in hospitals and community settings.

With a commitment to producing competent, compassionate healthcare providers, IHS graduates are highly sought after and make significant contributions to improving health outcomes throughout Botswana and the region.

Facilities

IHS boasts modern facilities designed to provide students with an optimal learning environment. These include:

  • State-of-the-art simulation laboratories that replicate real healthcare environments
  • Well-equipped skills laboratories for hands-on practice of clinical procedures
  • Modern lecture halls with audiovisual equipment
  • Computer laboratories with access to digital learning resources
  • Well-stocked library with extensive healthcare literature and online databases
  • Student residential facilities at all campuses
  • Recreation centers for student well-being
  • Clinical training sites at major hospitals and health facilities across Botswana
