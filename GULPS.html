About GULPS

Gaborone University College of Law and Professional Studies stands as a beacon of legal education excellence in Botswana. Founded with the vision of producing competent, ethical, and innovative legal professionals, GULPS combines rigorous academic training with practical experience to prepare graduates for the complexities of modern legal practice.
