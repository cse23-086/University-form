About BUAN

The Botswana University of Agriculture and Natural Resources (BUAN) is a specialized institution dedicated to advancing agricultural sciences and natural resource management in Botswana and the region. Established to address the critical need for skilled professionals in agriculture and environmental sectors, BUAN has become a leading center for agricultural education, research, and innovation.

With state-of-the-art research facilities, demonstration farms, and extensive field stations, BUAN provides students with hands-on experience in modern agricultural practices, sustainable farming techniques, and natural resource conservation. The university's strong focus on practical learning ensures graduates are well-prepared to tackle real-world challenges in food security, environmental sustainability, and rural development.

BUAN's commitment to research excellence has made it a key contributor to agricultural innovation in Southern Africa, with ongoing projects in climate-smart agriculture, livestock management, and water resource conservation.

Academic Programs

BUAN offers comprehensive programs designed to meet the evolving needs of the agricultural and natural resources sectors:

Crop Science

Focus on crop production, plant breeding, soil fertility, and sustainable farming systems for food security.

Animal Science

Comprehensive livestock management, animal nutrition, breeding, and veterinary care programs.

Agricultural Economics

Business aspects of agriculture, market analysis, policy development, and rural development economics.

Natural Resources Management

Conservation, sustainable use of natural resources, wildlife management, and environmental protection.

Agricultural Engineering

Farm mechanization, irrigation systems, post-harvest technology, and agricultural infrastructure.

Food Science & Technology

Food processing, preservation, quality control, and value addition in agricultural products.

Research & Innovation

BUAN is at the forefront of agricultural research in Botswana, conducting cutting-edge studies that directly impact food security and environmental sustainability:

Key Research Areas

  • Climate-Smart Agriculture and Drought-Resistant Crops
  • Sustainable Livestock Production Systems
  • Water Resource Management and Irrigation Technology
  • Soil Health and Fertility Management
  • Agricultural Value Chain Development
  • Wildlife Conservation and Human-Wildlife Conflict Management

The university operates several research stations across different agro-ecological zones, providing diverse environments for field research and technology testing.
