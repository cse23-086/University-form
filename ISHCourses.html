General Admission Requirements

Academic Points: Minimum of 34 points required in the best six BGCSE subjects for all undergraduate programs at IHS University.
English Proficiency: Minimum grade C in English Language is mandatory for all programs, demonstrating adequate communication skills.
Mathematics Requirement: Mathematics is required for most programs, with grade B needed for Computing and some Business programs, grade C for others.
Science Subjects: Biology and/or Chemistry are required for health-related programs, with grade C or better in relevant science subjects.
Alternative Entry: Diploma holders and mature students (25+ years) with relevant work experience may apply through alternative assessment procedures.
