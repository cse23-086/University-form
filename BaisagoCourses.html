General Admission Information

Standard Entry: Minimum 24 points in best six BGCSE subjects (or equivalent qualification)
Language Requirement: English Language proficiency with minimum grade C
Mature Entry: Applicants 25+ years with relevant work experience may qualify
Advanced Standing: Diploma holders may qualify for credit transfer and reduced study time
