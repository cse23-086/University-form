About Baisago University

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality higher education that prepares students for successful careers in a rapidly evolving global economy. We focus on practical skills, innovative thinking, and ethical leadership.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution for business and technology education in Southern Africa, recognized for academic excellence, industry partnerships, and graduate success.

Why Choose Us?

With campuses in Gaborone and Francistown, we offer flexible study options, experienced faculty, modern facilities, and strong industry connections that ensure our graduates are job-ready.

Academic Programs

Business Management

Comprehensive business education covering management principles, strategic planning, and entrepreneurship with real-world applications.

Information Technology

Cutting-edge IT programs including software development, network administration, cybersecurity, and digital innovation.

Public Administration

Prepare for careers in government and public service with courses in policy analysis, public finance, and governance.

Human Resources

Learn modern HR practices including talent management, organizational behavior, and strategic human capital development.

Marketing

Digital marketing, brand management, consumer behavior, and market research in the modern business landscape.

Accounting & Finance

Professional accounting education with focus on financial analysis, auditing, and corporate finance management.

Admissions Information

Entry Requirements

Minimum BGCSE with Grade C or better in English and Mathematics. Specific programs may have additional requirements. Mature students and international applicants welcome.

Application Process

Submit completed application form with certified copies of certificates, ID, and passport photos. Applications accepted throughout the year with multiple intake periods.

Financial Aid

Various payment plans available including installment options. Government loan schemes accepted. Merit-based scholarships for outstanding students.
